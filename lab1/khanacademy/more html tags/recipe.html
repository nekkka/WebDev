Hamburger

  • Time: 35 minutes
  • Serves: for 1 people
Ingredients Quantity
Burger Buns 1
Ground beef 1
Cheddar cheese 1
Tomatoes 1
Ketchup 1

Step 1: Gather ground beef, burger buns, cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, condiments (ketchup, mustard, mayo), salt, and pepper.

Step 2: Shape the ground beef into patties, season with salt and pepper, then grill or pan-fry until cooked to your desired level of doneness. Toast the burger buns, assemble the burger with cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and condiments, then serve hot.
